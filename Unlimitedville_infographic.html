Detailed Findings & Recommendations

HP-01

Homepage: Value Proposition

Problem:

Key benefit icons are too small, and the mention of a data cap creates confusion against the "Unlimited" headline.

Solution:

Increase the visual weight of benefit icons. Simplify the data promise on the homepage and move specifics to the plans page to reduce initial friction.

HP-02

Homepage: Confidence & Trust

Problem:

The single, easily missed testimonial at the bottom of the page is insufficient for building trust with new visitors.

Solution:

Create a prominent "What Our Customers Say" section with a carousel of testimonials and an aggregate star rating to immediately build social proof.

PP-01

Plans Page: Friction

Problem:

Plans are presented in separate text blocks, forcing users to scroll and memorize details, which complicates comparison and decision-making.

Solution:

Redesign the section into a side-by-side comparison table. This makes key features like price and speed easily scannable and comparable.

PP-02

Plans Page: Clarity

Problem:

Add-ons are presented ambiguously after plan selection, leaving users unsure of when and how to add them to their order.

Solution:

Integrate add-ons as optional checkboxes presented immediately after a user selects a plan, clarifying the checkout flow.
